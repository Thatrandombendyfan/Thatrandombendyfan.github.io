Bendy and the Ink Machine

About

Bendy and the ink machine is an indie horror game. The game is about a formor worker of Joey Drew Studios named Henry that has come back after leaving for thirty years. But, when he came back, not everything is what it seems. The studio was abandoned and there are ink creatures everywhere. The links in the top right corner is showing the characters to the game, Bendy and the Ink Machine. In chapter one, it has Henry, Alpha Bendy, Beta Bendy, and Ink Bendy. In chapter two, Sammual "Sammy" Lawrence, Beta Searchers, and Searchers. Chapter three, there is Boris, Twisted Alice, Piper, Stricker, Fisher, Swollen Searchers, and the Projectionist. Chapter four contains, Brute Boris, Lost Ones, and Bertrum Piedmont. Chapter five contains Beast Bendy, Allison Angel, Tom Boris, and the Giant Hand. Each one will show how they look and how they are. In the "Other" tab, It will show Cartoon Bendy, Cartoon Boris, Cartoon Alice, Barley, Charlie, and Edgar, along with wepons and items you will use.
